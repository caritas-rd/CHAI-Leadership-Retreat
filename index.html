About the Programme

A retreat designed for healthcare leaders

Bringing together the academic excellence of IIM Kozhikode and the deep industry experience of QuidelOrtho — exclusively for senior leaders across the CHAI Hospitals Network.

"This two-day programme offers healthcare leaders a rare opportunity to pause, reflect, and reconnect with the principles of patient-centred care — within a structured academic setting shaped for mission hospitals."

I
Tailored for Mission Hospitals
Every session draws on real-world examples relevant to faith-based and mission-driven healthcare — not generic corporate frameworks.
II
Academic Rigour
Faculty from IIM Kozhikode bring structured, evidence-based perspectives on leadership, quality, and strategy.
III
Industry Expertise
QuidelOrtho brings deep healthcare industry insights alongside faculty sessions for a well-rounded learning experience.
IV
Peer Networking
Connect and exchange with senior leaders from across the CHAI network — in a warm, collegial residential setting.
AI
Special Module: Digital Transformation & AI in Healthcare
A dedicated session on navigating emerging technologies — with practical, grounded guidance for mission hospital leadership.
Programme Structure

Four pillars of leadership excellence

Each module is designed to build a distinct leadership capability — together, they form a complete foundation for patient-centred hospital leadership.

I
Patient Experience & Customer Centricity
Focus on creating lasting value and reducing patient anxiety. Build service frameworks that reflect the dignity and care at the heart of your hospital's mission.
IIM Faculty
II
Quality & Service Excellence
Master quality standards and benchmarking essential for maintaining safety, dignity, and trust — with practical tools applicable to mission hospital settings.
Industry Insights
III
Financial Grounding for Leaders
Develop the financial literacy to ask the right strategic questions — sustainability dashboards and controls designed specifically for mission hospital priorities.
IIM Faculty
IV
Strategic Orientation & Leadership
Navigate trade-offs with clarity, build actionable roadmaps, and develop the strategic confidence to lead through modern healthcare challenges.
Industry Insights
+
Digital Transformation & AI in Healthcare
A focused special module on AI trends, digital tools, and practical roadmaps — grounded in the realities of mission hospital leadership.
